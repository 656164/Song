About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival action game where you face endless waves of monsters in an ever-expanding world. As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience points that allow you to level up and obtain new weapons and abilities.

The game features roguelite elements with randomized power-ups and enemies, ensuring that each playthrough offers a unique experience. How long can you survive against the monster hordes?

With its addictive gameplay loop and progressive difficulty curve, Monster Survivors provides hours of entertainment as you strive to improve your survival time and discover new character builds and strategies.

How to Play

Monster Survivors features intuitive controls that are easy to learn but challenging to master:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map
  • Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies
  • Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters
  • Level up to choose new weapons or upgrade existing ones
  • Collect power-ups and items that appear throughout the game
  • Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters

The key to success is finding the right combination of weapons and upgrades that complement each other, while continuously moving to avoid being surrounded by enemies.
